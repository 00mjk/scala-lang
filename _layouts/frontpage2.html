Scala began life in 2003, created by Martin Odersky and his research group at EPFL next to Lake Geneva and the Alps, in Switzerland. Scala has since grown into a mature open source programming language, used by hundreds of thousands of developers, and is developed and maintained by scores of people all over the world.
